ResourceVerification
viewAll Verify that the text next to the close button is translated.
viewDetails Verify that the text after the message description is translated.
refresh Verify that the text at the right bottom corner is translated.

Verify that the text in the tooltip is translated.
hideDetails Toggle the message title to expand the message description. Hover mouse over the message title to show the tooltip.
Verify that the text in the tooltip is translated.
