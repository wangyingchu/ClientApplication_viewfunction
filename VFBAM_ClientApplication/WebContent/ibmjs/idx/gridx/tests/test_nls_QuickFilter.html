ResourceVerification
filter Verify that the hint message "Filter" in the quick filter box is translated.
clear Type something in the quick filter box. Mouse hover the cross icon (x) at the right corner of the quick filter box, wait for the tooltip to popup. Verify the "Clear Filter" tooltip is translated.
apply Mouse hover the button to the right of the quick filter box, wait for the tooltip to popup. Verify the "Apply Filter" tooltip is translated.
